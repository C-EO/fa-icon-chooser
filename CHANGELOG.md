## [0.4.1](https://github.com/FortAwesome/fa-icon-chooser/releases/tag/0.4.1) - 2022-08-31

### Changed

## [0.4.0](https://github.com/FortAwesome/fa-icon-chooser/releases/tag/0.4.0) - 2022-08-31

### Added

### Changed
